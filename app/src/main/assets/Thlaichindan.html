Dragon Fruit

Chin Hun leh Chin dan

  1. A zar tana phun nun hi tihpun dan tlangpui a ni a. A zar tihpun tur chu a chang tha leh hrisel kung a mi tur a ni.
  2. A chi (seeds) atangin a tihpun theih a, a chi lakna tur a rah chu a kungah hmin tha tak a zuah tur a ni. A chi lakchhuah hnuah pho ro a tha a, leitha taka kui a tha. A zar phun aiin a puitling har hle zawk.
  3. A zar cut leh phun hi kum tluan in a tih theih.
  4. Huanah leh pot-ah hlawhtling takin a chin theih.

Tui leh Leitha pek dan

  1. Nitin tui pek a harsat avangin kar khat danah tui hneh taka pek chuan a tha hle tho a ni.
  2. Bawngek leitha 15kgs bul khat atan kum tin pek theih a tha, Urea 50 gms, SSP 50 gms leh MOP 100 gms te hi bul khat-ah vawikhat pek atan a tawk a, May, August leh October thlaah te pek thin tur a ni. Hei bakah hian leitha inpawlh sa NPK hi hman uar a tha bawk.
  3. A rah dawn hnaih April-ah leh a rah zawh hnua leitha leh tui pek nachang hriat a tha.

Sik leh Sa

  1. 10˚C - 35˚C inkarah a tha duh ber.
  2. Hmun hnawng leh rei tak dai tlak lohna hmunah a hrisel ber.
  3. Ram vawt lutuk vur tlakna hmunah chuan a rah tha thei lo.

Chin tur zat

    Ban/bul pakhatah Dragon fruit chi 2/3 zel phun tur a ni.

Thar theih zat leh a chi hrang

Acre 1 hmunah 20-24 Qtl a thar thei, enkawl dan in a zir thei hle.

Variety

Dragon fruit ah hian variety hrang 25 lai a awm. Bloody Mary, Costa Rican Sunset, Alice, American Beauty, Dark Star, Delight leh Yellow.

Enkawl zui tur

  1. A pengte paih then thin tur (pruning)
  2. A kawkalh leh nghet taka do tur.
  3. Mau emaw thing hmun thli dan (wind break) zuah/phun tur.

Thlai Venhim

Dragon fruit natna zingah Anthracnose leh Red rust te hi an lai a, fungicide a kah tur a ni.

Balhla

Phun hun

March - April hi Mizorama balhla phun hun a ni. A bul an phun thin (Sucker). Balhla phun tur lakin a te vak tur a ni lo,A lian a tha zel.

Khur laih

Bi tinah a khur ft. 1 leh a chanve a zau leh ft chanve a thuk laih hram hram a tha.

Tui pek

Balhla phun hun hi thal khawro lai a ni a, theih hram chuan tum 2 tum 3 chu tui pe thei ila a tha.

Leitha pek

    Phun rual emaw phun hlimah bul khat zelah FYM kg 12, N kg 110, P kg 120, K kg330 kum 2 na atangin FYM kg 15, N kg 150, P kg 200, K kg 500 Kum tin pek angai.

Sik leh Sa

Variety hrang hrang awm a, variety a zirin hmun tinah a chin theih

Chin tur zat

Chi hrang 3, a sang, a laihawl leh a hniam a awm a. A bi khat dan pawh a inanglo. A tlar ft .10 a inhlat a tlara a bi ft. 8 a inhlatin acre 1 ah bi 588 a leng thei.

A Chi hrang

Mizoramah Balhla chi hrang 4 lai kan nei. Banria, Banbawl, lawngbalhla leh Vaibalhla kual (cavennoi).

Ven leh enkawl dan

  1. Balhla natna lar te chu- A hnah de leh ro (Sigatoka), Balhla kung chhungril tawih leh a hnahro(Wet) hi a ni a, a enkawl nan Carbedazim @2gm leh tui litre 1 chawhpawlh a kah tur a ni.
  2. Balhla zik bawrchhawr, thangthei lo (Bunchy top) a awm chuan kar a, vau a paih tur a ni.
  3. Balhla eichhetu zinga lar ber (Balhla tam an tih awm tirtu) chu Tuaingawt dum a ni. Balhla bulah a tui a, pangngang a keu a, pangngang chu balhla kung chhungah a lut a, a balhla a hreuh kaw chuk a, a buhchium hnenah Tuaingawt dum a lo piang a, chu chuan balhla kung chhung a hreuh kaw zui bawk. A suat nan Phorate 10G-chu balhla zik leh a kauah thliah hlek hlek tur a ni.
  4. Balhla hnah leh balhla hel dit rangtu Keltelaimawm (Fruit carring beetle) suat nan Monocotophos 2 ml leh tui litre 1 chawhpawlh a kah tur a ni.
Grape

Chin Hun leh Chin dan

  1. A tang vih nuna chin thin a nih avangin Jan. - Dec. ah an vih phun thin.
  2. Khur : 3ft x 3ft x 3ft a laih tur.
  3. Head System : A bi kar inhlat zawng 1.8 m x 2.8 m, Acre (tin khat ) hmunah 850-900 a leng.
  4. Bower System : Thirzai zam khawkalh 2 - 2.5 metres vela sangah a bi kar inhlat zawng 3m x 3m vel a tha.
  5. Kniffin System : Metre khat vela sang banah thir hrui, a zam-na siam.
  6. Overhead trellis : Phun kar inhlat zawng 3m x 3m a tawk.

Tui leh Leitha pek dan

  1. Feb-Apr inkar, khaw ro zual lai hian kar khat ah tui vawi 1 tal pe ila,hei hi a chawr tan lai a ni bawk a.
  2. Bawngek leh Compost bakah Urea; DAP (SSP) leh MOP (NPK) hi a chawr tan leh a rah lawh zawh hlimah pe thin ila, tui pawh pe ila a tha.

Sik leh Sa

  1. A hnah tlakkawlh nan Thalsik boruak a mamawh.
  2. Leilung chi hrang hranga chin theih a ni nain 5.5PH - 7.5PH hi lei thur leh a al tehnaah him tawka ngaih a ni.

Chin tur zat

  1. Nursery bed-ah 30cm X 100cm danah a mit pakhat langa vih phun tur a ni.
  2. Head system 850, Bower System 460, Overhead Trellis-460.

Thar theih zat leh a chi hrang

Mizorama kan Grape chin Bangalore Blue hi kung 1 ah Kg 15-20 thar thei tura hisap a ni a, chindan chi hrang hrang Acre 1 a kung awm zat hian puntir ila, a hriat theih. (850 x 15 = 12750 Kgs, i.e. 127 Qntls)

Thlai Venhim dan

  1. Anthracnose natna leh powdery mildew natna laka ven nan Bavistin leh Bordeux Mixture 1% hmanga kah thin tur a ni.
  2. Rannung zingah Flea beetle, thrips leh scales ten grape an tichhia in insecticides a kah a ngai.

Variety

  1. Vitis Lubrusca - Wine Grapes, Red Table Grapes, Purple/Blue Grapes,Bangalore Blue
  2. Vitis Vinifera - Red/White Grapes (Wine), Red/White Table Grapes.
Buh thar rang

Kui, tuh leh phun hun

  1. May thla chhung hi buhchi kûi hun a ni.
  2. Ruahtui awmdan a zirin June thla tir thleng a kui theih.
  3. April leh May thla chhung hi thingtlang lovah chuan buh tuh hun a ni.
  4. Buh tiak ni 20 a upa phun sawn tur.

Tui / Leitha pek dan

  1. Buh phun hlim kha a nun chian hma chu tui inches 1 atanga inches 2 vel chim tir tur a ni.
  2. Chumi hnuah chuan a inhampuar thlengin inches 2 vela thukin tui a chim tir leh tur a ni
  3. Chutah senghma ni 10 atanga ni 15 inkar velah tui paih tur a ni.
  4. Ran ek leitha emaw hnimhnah up tawih leitha emaw quintal 40-60 vel buh tin khat hmuna zauvah buh phun hma chawlhkar 4 leh 6 inkar vela pek a tha. Fertilizer chu Urea kg 83, DAP kg 65 MOP kg 60 acre khat a zauva pek atan a tawk.

Sik leh Sa

  1. A chi kuitiah atanga a thar thlenga a boruak mamawh dan chu 21°C leh 37° C inkar a ni.
  2. A par laiin 26.5°C leh 29.5°C inkar a lum nise a tha.
  3. A hmin atanga a seng thlengin 20°C- 25°C a tawk.
  4. Tlangram lo atan chuan kan ruahtui dawn dan pangngai hi a tawk viau.

Buh chi mamawh zat

  1. Buh fang sin deuh a nihin hetiang buhchi kuangah hian kg 12-14 acre 1 atan kui ila, a fang a len deuh chuan kg 16 kûi a tawk ang.
  2. SRI atan chuan kg 2 acre 1 atan a tawk.

Variety leh a Chi hrang

    Variety hrang tam tak zingah HYV leh Hybrid chin a tha. Certified seed ngei nise. CAUR 1, DRRH-2 etc.

Hlothlawh

Fai taka hlothlawh hi thingtlang lovah leh leiletah an pawimawh hle. Hlo tiah hma leilet kawi hnuah emaw buh phun hnua hman chi hlo tur hman a tha. Butachlor, Pretilachlore te. A hnuah hlo tiah tawh hnuah hlo hnahbial thahna tur kah leh tur. 24D te pandimethalin te.

Thlai venhim dan

  1. Buh hnah de (Brown Spot) , Buhkung duk (Stalk rot), Buh kung tui chim chin a duk a, meihawl anga dum (Stem Rot) a awm chuan Blast natna enkawlna damdawi ang hi kah tur a ni.
  2. Buh kung dawttu Katnelh(Plant Hoppers),Buh lairil khertu pangngang (Stem Borer)Buh hnah sehchhuma hlawm siam tu pangngang (Caseworm), Buh hnah khawrtu pangngang (Leaf Folder) Buh dawttu thlangdar (Rice bug)te hi leileta buh eichhetu rannung lar zual a ni. A tul dan a zirin Rannungthahna hlo- Quinalphos, Phosphamidon, Dimulthoa @ 2 ml leh tui litre-1 chawhpawlh a kah tur a ni.
  3. Thingtlang lo ah chuan Buh zung rah (Rice Root aphits), Vual leh Sairilhrik (Flea beetles), Buh hnah khawrtu pangngang (Leaf Folder) leh Buh vui dawttu thlangdar ( Rice Bug) te hi buh eichhetu rannung lar zual a ni, A chunga mi ang khian kah tur a ni.
Vaimim (Maize)

Chin Hun leh Chin dan

  1. Vaimim hi kangvarah, March leh April thla ah tuh tur a ni.
  2. October - November a tuh hian March- April ah a seng theih.
  3. Phaizawl leiletah pawh an ching thin

Tui leh leitha pek dan

  1. A chhâwn thlak lai leh a fang insiam tan lai hi a mamawh ang tawk tui a hmuh tul zual hun a ni.
  2. Tui a tam lutuk erawh chuan paih kang a tul a, tui paihna ngaihtuah vat a tul.
  3. Leilet zawlah chuan chutiang tui paihna tur metre 6 danah ft 1 vela zau, ft ½ vela thuk siam tur.
  4. Vaimim hmunah darkar 4 - 5 bak tui a tling tur a nilo. Vaimim hmun atan eng lei pawh a tha deuh zel mai kan ti nain tlak lei (clayey) leh leime (sandy) chu thlan loh tur.
  5. Tin, pH 6.5-7.5 hi a tawk.

    Fertilizer pek tur zat (Kg/Acre)
    Urea - 52 Urea- 56
    SSP - 100 emaw DAP- 35
    MOP - 27 MOP -27

  6. Ran zun-ek leitha, hnimhnah tawih leitha te leh fertilizer-te hi pe ila, thlai in a ngeih a, fertilizer chauh pek aiin a tha zawk.
  7. Organic leitha chu Tin 1 (1 acre) hmun a zau ah quintal 20 pe thei ila a tha.

A bikar

  1. Thingtlangah pawh len nei a chin a tha. A bikar hi ft 2 - 21/2 vel nise.
  2. Square emaw a tlar inkar chu ft 3 vel pawh nise.

Sik leh Sa

  1. Vaimim chin zawng zawng za a 85 (%) zet tur hi nipui lai (monsoon/kharif) a chin a ni.
  2. Zan boruak 15.6 °C aia a hniamah a thang tha thei lo.
  3. Mizoram hmun tam berah fur laiin Thingtlang lovah vaimim an ching.
  4. Vur tlakna hmunah chin loh tur a ni.

Thlai chi mamawh zat

  1. Tin 1 (1 acre) hmun a zau ah 7.2-8 kg a tawk.
  2. Ranchaw atan kan ching a nih erawh chuan kg 25-30 chin tur a ni.
  3. Bi khatah fang 3 vel thlak ila a tawk

Thar theih zat Sik leh Sa leh Variety

HQPM-5, Vijay Composite,Sangam,Navjot,Chakra,Sweet Corn,Pop Corn, Qaty Corn.Hybrid leh Certified HYV chin ngei atha.Tin Mizo Vaimim zingah mimpui leh mimban tha tak tak a awm.

Hlothlawh

Vaimim hmun hi thlawhfai a ngai nain hnim awm azirin a hautak lo. Kuta thlawh bakah hlo tiah hmaa kah chi hlo tur Simazine/Atrazine te, a tiah hnua kah chi 2,4D te hmang ila a tha.

Thlai venhim

Vaimim hi ven uluk angai.A kung nget, a kawm leh fang nget emaw rulte, tunlai phei chuan FAW kan hre lar leh ta.In thlahdah lova kan thlai kan vil angai Systematic + contact insecticide kah atha.FAW hi sahbawn tuia control theih mahse dal tawk kan zawng tur ani. Natna pawh a tam , thlai chi fungicide a bual hmasak vek tur.

Thlai seng

Kan chin chhan azirin a kawm keuva en ve reng a ngai. Kawm chhum leh rawh turte dah ro turte pawh enchung renga seng tur ani. Dah rova a fang nuai tur chu a tui/hnawng pai za a 20% vel a nih in seng tur.

Behlawi (Cowpea)

Chin hun leh Chin dan

April - May thla velah lovah leh leipui emaw huanah kan chingin, tui pek tur a awm chuan a chin theih. Mizoramah chuan behlawi kan chin chhan leh eidan chu an hnah atan leh arah no eiah a ni ber. A chi/amu kan ei lem lo.Leiletah pawh ching lovin thingtlang lovah kan ching ber.

Leitha pek

Urea , DAP + MOP te pek atha. A hnah kan duh avang hian Nitrogen lam pek a tha a, a zung a tlo a ngaih bawk avangin DAP hi a tha viau.

A chi mamawh zat

Acre 1 a zauvah 5-7 kgte an ching in, kg 8-10 lai ching an awm. A bi zin dan a zir te, a variety te pawhin a zir. A bi tlar hi fit 11/2 - 2 nise, a bi kar chu fit 1 nise.

Variety

  1. Ram lumah acre khat zel ah 180 - 270 kg te a thar theih.
  2. A thar rang hi ni 50 hnu ah a thar theih a, a thar tlai hi thla nga (5) velah an thar ber.
  3. Black-eye pea, China pea, Kaffir pea, Southern bean, Marble pea, Yard-long bean, Asparagus bean, Bodi bean, Snake bean te a awma.Mizorama kan behlawi chin chu fur behlawi,a mudumte a rah sen leh hringte arah sei chi pawh kan ching ve tan a, local an ni hlawm.

Thlai ven him

A chi hi fungicide leh Rhizobium a treat a tha. Chu chuan fur laia lawng lakah pawh a veng thei. Caterpillar leh aphid a awm chuan insecticide a kah tur.

A thar hlawk lam

Acre 1 a a hnah thar zat chhinchhiah a awm lo vang.Arah erawh chu quintal 20-30 a ni thei.A mu ro erawh chu quintal 4-7 a thar thei.A chinna leilung ,enkawl dan leh variety a zir tein a danglam thei.

Behliang (Arhar)

Chin hun leh Chin dan

Thingtlang lovah chuan April - May ah an tuh a, June leh July ah pawh an tuh.

Leitha pek dan

  1. Urea 13 Kgs leh SSP 88 Kgs acre 1 a zauvah.
  2. Ngun taka lei nen chawhpawlh a tha.
  3. Rhizobium chi nena pawlh chuan Urea 9 Kgs leh SSP 88 Kgs chauh hman tur. DAP kan pek chuan kg 40 a tawk.

Sik leh Sa

Behliang (Arhar) hi ruahtui tlemna hmunah pawh a tha duh.

A chi mamawh zat

  1. Acre 1 hmunah 5-6kg a tawk.
  2. Feet 2 1/2 inhlata chin nise.
  3. A tlar a nih chuan a tlar ft. 2 leh a chanve a inhlat, a bi ft.1 inkar

Hlo thlawh

A chi thlak hnuah tum 2 vel hlothlawh angai amaherawhchu lo hnim dan azirin thlawh tur.

Variety

Prabhat, UPAS, kan thlai chin hi HYV, natna leh rannung chimawm tuar zo deuh ni zel se a tha.

A thar theih zat

Acre 1 hmunah 5-6 qtl a thar theih.

Thlai ven dan

  1. Kutdurh hian a par a ei vangin a theih ang tawka tam man a, hmeh hlum tur a ni.
  2. A rah hunah a rah apet/khertu pangngang in a mu a eichhe thei.
  3. Kutdurh leh pangngang suat nan Qunalphos @3ml leh tui litre khat chawhpawlhin a kuhmum lai atangin kah tur a ni.
  4. Behliang rah hi a nget duh a, ven uluk a ngai.
French Bean

Chin hun leh Chin dan

  1. September-November rabi tan, Kharif tan Feb-Apl thla thleng.
  2. A zamlo chi (Dwarf Bean) chu a bi inches 6-8 danah a tlar inches 18-24 a inhlatin chin tur
  3. A zam chi (pole) chu a bi inches 15 vel danah a tlar inches 18-24 a inhlatin chin tur

Tui leh Leitha pek dan

  1. February thla a chin chuan tui pek tam a ngai.
  2. Luikawr phai leh hnawngah tui pek a ngai lo.
  3. Vaivut leh lei tak inchawhpawlh lei thawp a tha. pH 5-6 vel a tawk.
  4. Urea, DAP leh MOP hi thlai chi thlak ruala a chi hrul ah leh tlema thuk zawk a leia phul tur. Rhizobium leh phosphotica a a chi bual chuan N leh P a sukiang hle. Urea kg 50, DAP kg 52 leh MOP kg 27 acre 1 hmuna pek atan.

Sik leh Sa

Khawlum lutuk lo leh vawt lutuk a duh lo. A kawm insiam nan 15.6-21.1˚C a tawk. Motor chana aiin khawlumah a tha duh.

A chi mamawh zat

  1. A zam lo chi (Dwarf Bean)- Tin/Acre khat hmuna zauah a chi kg 34-36.
  2. A zam chi (Pole type)-Tin/Acre khat hmuna zau ah a chi Kg. 10-12.

Bean chi hrang leh an thar theih zat

  • A zam chi - Kentuckey wonder. Scarlet Emperor. Kawm tawi, kawm thau
  • A zam lo chi - Contender, Tendergreen, Giant Stringless, Bountiful.
  • A kawm hringin 32-48 acre hmunah a thar thei.

Ven leh enkawl dan

  1. A bul thlawh fai reng leh tui tling theilo tura siam tur
  2. A hnah hnuaiah phut sen (Rust) a awm thin
  3. A hnah rau leh a rah dum thliah thliah (Anthracnose) in a bawm thei.
  4. Natna laka ven nan Carbendazim / Moncozeb @2gm leh furi litre 1 pawlh a kah tur a ni.
  5. Rannung Aphid, Sassids, Beetles leh a hri Anthracnose roorrot, wilt leh leaf spot laka ven angai. French bean hi thlasik thlai ani a, chi engemaw zat a awm a, a zam chi (pole type) leh zam lo chi (bush/dwarf type) a awm. A kung a bikin a zamchiin ruah nan a ngeih lo, a rah pawh a mawi thei lo.
Kelek Chana (Gram, Chick pea)

Kelek Chana hi be, pulses kan tih zing ami thlai tangkai leh tha tak a ni. A pawimawh hle.

Chin hun leh Chin dan

  1. October leh November thla hi chin hun chu a ni.
  2. Thlai chi chu a tlarin ching ila, a tlar inkar chu 30-45 cm (1-1½ ft) a inhlat nise.

Tui pek dan

  1. Tui pek tlem tur.
  2. A par laiin tui pek loh tur a ni.
  3. Thlai chin dawn a pek a tha.

Sik leh Sa

  1. Ruahtui tam a mamawh lo
  2. Thlasik laia chin tur a ni.
  3. Kum khatah ruahtui 600 - 900mm atawk.
  4. Tui pek theih chuan a tha.

A chi mamawh zat

Acre/Tin khat hmunah Chana chi Kg 32 vel chin tur a ni. A tlarin, a tlar inkar ft 1-1½ vel nise a tawk.

Thar theih zat leh a Chi hrang

  1. Desi or Brown Gram (Cicer arietinum L) : Hei hi Kelek Chana kan tih tak a hi a ni.
  2. Kabuli or White Gram (Cicer kabulium) : A chi (a mu) hi a var emaw, a eng dal emaw a ni thung. A thar hlawk vak lo, variety leh chin uluk dan in a zir thung. Acre khatah 6-8 a thar theia, qtl 12 pawh a tling thei.

Thlai vendan

  1. Rannung :Pangngang suat nan rannung thahna hlo kah tur a ni. Cutworm leh Pod boree te hi insect tichhe tu an ni.
  2. Natna : Damdawiin a chi bual a, a kung chunga natna lo lan veleh emaw a hma pawha fungicide kah tur.
  3. Fungicide hman tur chi hrang hrang a awm a, mithiam rawn zel a tha.

Leitha pek

Nitrogen mamawh za a 75 a intum thei. Phosphorus a mamawh tama, nodule insiam hmaa N hman tirnan DAP kg 40-60 acre 1 hmuna pek a tha. Potassium a mamawh vak lo.

Hlo thlawh

A kung a te in a hniama, a awmnem avangin hnim dip a ngaitheilo. Chuvangin hlo thlawhfai viau a tha. Hnim tiah hmaa hman chi hlo tui pendimethalin emaw Butechler a kah a tha.

Thlai seng

A hnah sen buang/uk rawnga a lo awmin seng a hun. A rah kuta mal emaw a kung pawhphawi leh favaha zai chhuma hruih leh Silpauline-a ni 5-7 vel pho in mal tur.

Matar Chana (Pea, Field Pea)

Chin hun

October thla tir atanga November thla laihawl a chin tur. Thlasik thlai a nia, tlangramah March-May a ching an awm.

Chinna leilung leh tui pek dan

  1. Thlai chin laiin lei a hnawngtha tur a ni.
  2. A tiah hnuah tui vawi hnih pe ila, chu chu chin atanga ni 45-50 naah leh ni 80 na velah nise.
  3. A vawihnihna chu a kawm a inhampuar tanah pek leh tur a ni .
  4. Tui chim emaw tui tling emaw a ngeih lo.
  5. A chinna atan a lei duh tawk chu pH 5.5 - 7.5 a ni. Leitha, tui tlin theih lohna hmuna chin a tha.
  6. Tui lak nena chawm tur anih chuan a hmun buatsaih laiin uluk taka lei hrut rual tur a ni.

Sik leh Sa

  1. Motor chana chi hian atiah na atan 22℃- 25℃ vel boruak a duh.
  2. A hnuah a par lai leh a insiam lai velin boruak vawt leh temperature hniam deuh 15℃ - 18℃ inkar vel a tawk.
  3. Mizoram ah chuan thlasik laia chin tur pawimawh, vur tla pawh a tuar thei.

Chin dan leh a chi mamawh zat

  1. Thlai chi chu a tlara tuh tur a ni.
  2. A tlar inkar hi ft 1 ½ a inhlat nisela.
  3. A bi inkar hi inches 2-4 a inhlat ni bawk sela.
  4. A bi thukzawng hi inch 2 pel lo sela. A chi hi variety a zirin acre 1 ah kg 24 vel a ni a, hei aia tlem 18-20 kg vel pawh atawk thei.

Matar chana chi hrangte

  1. Early Badger: A chi sawng leh chuar, a kung hniam (dwarf), ni 55-65 a thar thei.
  2. Arkel: A kung hniam, thar hlawk, thar rang, a kawm hringa seng theih. Tin khat hmunah a kawm hringin qtl 34 a thar thei.
  3. Boneville: A chi sawng leh chuar, a thuaha a kawm insiam (double podder), tlema sang deuh (medium tall), ni 85 ah a thar thei a
  4. Early Giant: Thar rang, a kung a sang a, a kawm hring deuh, a rah sawng leh chuar a ni.

  5. . Khur khatin khum khat atana a mamawh zat - Bawngek leitha 10 - 15kg (tin khat vel), Neemcake 1.5 kg, Nitrogen (Urea-in) 50gms, Phosphorous (SSP/DAP in 30gms, Potash (MOP) 30gms.

  3. Seng hunah boruak lum leh ro ni thei se.

A Chi mamawh zat

Variety leh thar theih zat

  2. Sawhthing kg 1 zelah Nicoderma 10g tui litre 1-ah pawlhdal a, darkar khat tal a chi chiah nan hman tur.
  3. Nicoderma kg1 hi tui litre 100-a pawlhdalin sawhthing quital 1 chiah nan a hman theih. (Darkar khat tal chiah tur.
  4. Rannung ven nan Nico Neem 5ml tui litre 1-ah pawlhdalin ni 10 -15 dan zelah kah tur.
  5. Insect zingah shoot borer leh leaf roller te hian sawhthing beiin an tichhe thin. Insecticide a kah tur a ni.
  6. Natna zingah Rhizome (soft) rot leh leaf spot a lar a, chin dawnah a chi sawisak (treat) hmasak zel tur.
Purun (Onion)

A Chin Hun

Purun chihrang: Mizovin purun sen kan tih mai pawh hi chi hrang hrang alo awm. A sen dang, Thlasik(rabi) a chin chi, a dah rei theih ber. A sen tak emaw, sen duk, fur (Kharif) a chin chi, a dahrei theih vak loh. A var, a ru lova, a dahrei theih vak bik loh.

  1. September – October (a chi kui hun - Thlasik/Rabi)
  2. November - December (a chi kui hun - Nipui/Kharif)
  3. November – December (phun sawn hun - Thlasik/Rabi)
  4. January – February (a phun sawn hun - Nipui /Kharif)

A chi kuina siam leh a chi mamawh zat

  1. Acre khat hmuna phun tur kuina area 200 sq. metre
  2. A thlura siam tur a ni a, thlur khat zau zawng 1 metre
  3. A sei zawng Mamawh dan a zir leh a rem ang zelin
  4. Rihpawn san zawng 10-15cm or 4-6 inches
  5. A thlur (raised bed) inkar zau zawng 30cm or 1ft.
  6. Hectare 1 atan a chi 5-7 kg, Acre 1 atan 2-3 kg.

Sik leh Sa

  1. Boruak lum vak lo leh vawt vak lo a ngeih ber
  2. Kum khat chhunga ruahtui mamawh zat chu 650-750mm a ni.

A kuina tur leh a chi ngaizat

> Tin khat(acre) hmuna zau a phun tur kuina area - 200 sq. Metre, a chi ngaizat kg 2-3

A tiak phun sawn

Thlasik purun emaw fur purun tlai chu ni 45-50 a upa ah phun sawn tur. Fur purun erawh chu ni 35-40 a phun sawn tur purun tiak phun tur pawh sa chu 1/3 thuai bun tur. Upa lutuk leh naupang lutuk a tha lo. Tin zauah quintal 48-60 a thar thei.

A Vendan

  1. Purun hnah hi rannung te tak te te (Onion Thrips) in an ei rang thin. Profenofos 0.1%, Fipronil 0.1%, Carbosulfan 0.2% @2ml leh tui litre 1 a kah tur a ni.
  2. A kung hi natna (Purple Blotch) vangin a tawih thei. A ven nan Carbendizim/ Moncozeb @ 2 gm leh tui litre 1 chawhpawlh in kah tur a ni.

Tui pek

    Tui pek tur awmna ngeiah purun hmun siam tur. A kui tiahna hmunah pawh tuipek a ngai fo. Chinna hmun a zirin tui pek ngaihdan a in ang lo. Phunsawn hlimah ni 1 emaw ni 2 emaw dana pek a tha.

Purun variety

Agrifound light red, Agrifound Dark red, Bhima Kiran, Bhima Shakti, NHRDF Red-2, etc.

Leitha Pek Dan

A tira leia lehpawlh tur. Acre 1 a zauvah urea kg 21, DAP kg 35, MOP kg 40. Phun hnu ni 30 ah urea kg 30, phun hnu ni 45 ah urea kg 30 bawk pek leh tur.

Hlothlawh

Purun kung a sina, fai taka hlothlawh tur. Hlo tiah hmain hlo tur Oxyflurofen emaw Pendimethalin hman tur, A hnuah kuta thlawh tur.

Purun Seng

A hnah leh a par zatve vel a nguia a tlukin seng a hun. Furah chuan a bul entel angai. Seng hma lutuk a tha lo, a chawr duh.

Phoro

Purun chawhsa ni 3 vel leiah dah a, a hnuah ni 10 vel daihlim a dah leh phawta, a hnahro inch 1 vel zuaha thuai chhum tur.

Hmarchapui

A Kui Hun

  1. Kharif atan, hmun hniam deuh laiah chuan March - April thlaah an kuia.
  2. Rabi atan chuan Sept - Oct thla thleng a kui theih a ni.
  3. A kui dan chu vegitable dang kui ang hi a ni.
  4. Acre 1 a phun tur atan hmarcha chi 240 - 280 gm kui atawk. Chutiangin 100 gm atan 3-4 gm nise.
  5. Hmarcha tiak chu chawlhkar 4 - 5 a upa a nih in phunsawn theih a ni.
  6. Direct a tuh tur chu 40 - 45 cm a inhlatah tuh mai tur, a hnuah suat leh nise.

A Hmun Buatsaih

Tui tling thei lo tura ruahmanna tha tak siam ngei tur a ni. Uluk takin vawi 2-3 leh a, lung leh bawlhhlawh ang chi zawng zawngte thenfai a, dur tha taka siam tur a ni. A vang lam 5 ft, a dung erawh a rem dan anga seia hrut rualin a karah kalna emaw tui kalna tur tih khuar tur.

Phunsawn

Hmarcha tiak chawlhkar 4 - 5 a upa te chu dim taka lei nena pawh a, 1½ ft square a phun tur. 1 ft square pawhin an phun theih.

Thar Theih Zat

  1. California Wonder : Ram tin khat hmuna zauvah 40-60 qtl. a thar thei a ni.
  2. Bulnoj : A rah hi 8-10 cm a sei, tle nal han leh a kawr chhah hring duk a ni.Ram tin khat hmuna zauvah 40-48 qtls. vel a thar theih a ni.
  3. Pusa Deepti (K.Tri-1) :Ram tin / acre khat hmuna zauah 100-120 qtls. thleng a thar chhuah theih a ni.

Leitha Pek

  2. Leaf curl natna hi a lar bawk, kan hmuh apiang paiha, systemetic insecticide a thehdarhtu rannung kah tur.

Aieng (Tumeric)

Chin Hun leh Chin dan

March- inkarah a chi lin tur a ni. Phaizawlah lei leh tur. Kangvarah chuan tuthlawh hmanga lin mai tur.

Leitha pek dan

Acre 1 hmunah Urea kg24, DAP kg 26 leh MOP Kg 20 pek a tawk . Chin rualin DAP leh MOP pe vek ila, chin hnu ni 30 ah Urea chanve pein a chanve dang ni 90 ah pek leh tur.

  1. Boruak lum zawng 20-300C.
  2. Kum khata ruahtui tla 125 -200cm
  3. Leitha , dur deuh PH 4.5-7.5 a tha

A chi mamawh zat leh a bi kar

Ram tin khat(1acre) hmunah 8-10 qtls a tawk. A tlara phun chuan a tlar inkar hi inches 18-20 nise, a bi chu inch 6-8 inkar a tawk.

Thar theih zat

  1. Ram tin khat hmunah 80-100 qtls a thar thei.
  2. Aieng hi spice zinga thlai lar tak a ni a, Damdawi atan pawh a tangkai . Thla 7 ah a thar thei a, thla 9 vela thar pawh a awm. A kumhlun thei bawk. A chi hrang hrangte:-
    • Lakadong
    • Dzongu
    • Allepey
    • Armoov

Venhim Dan

  2. Neem 5ml/L hmanga ni 15 dan zelah aieng kah thin tur.
  3. Aieng quintal 1 chiah nan Trichoderma Viride Kg 1 tui litre 100-a pawlhin chin hmaa chiah tur a ni.
  4. Nicoderma leh organic leitha pawlha a kung bula phul pawh hi aieng ven nan a tha hle.
  5. Aieng natna lar ber chu a hnah val leh rau natna an ni a. Copper oxychloride (Blitox 50 emaw Fytolan) in thlai hnah val leh rau a lan tanin kah tur a ni.
Zikhlum (Cabbage)

Chin Hun leh Chin dan

  1. A kuina (nursery) ft 10 a sei leh ft. 3 a zau , inches 8 a rih pawn, hul tha takah pan rual taka gm40-50 vel kui tur .
  2. Kar 4-6 vel a nih a, hnah 3-4 a chhuah hnuah phunsawn tur a ni.

  3. Ruahsur lai leh khawchhiat nasat lutuk laia phun sawn loh a tha.

  5. Thingtlang lovah pawh kharif season-ah an chinga, a chi an theh mai.

Tui leh Leitha pek dan

  1. Tui pek dan mumal tak neih tur a ni a. Phun hlimah pek nghal tur.
  2. Pek loh reng hnua pek lehin a hlum a ti khiin a ti puak duh.
  3. Phunsawn hma kar 3-4 ah Bawngek leitha thehdarha, lei nena uluk taka chawhpawlh a, chinna tur leia lehhnan leh tur.
  4. Phunsawn hma kar 3-4 ah Slaked lime 4-5qtls acre 1 hmuna thehdarha , lei nen uluk taka chawhpawlha, a chinna tur leia lehhnan leh tur.
  5. Phun hunah SSP 185- 190 kgs acre1 hmunah lei nen uluk taka chawhpawlh tur.
  6. Phun hunah MOP 50kgs chinna lei nen uluk taka chawhpawlh tur.
  7. Urea chu kg 50, a zat ve phun rual velin , a chanve hmun hniha semin kg 12.5 hi phun hnu thla khatah leh adang chu a hlum tamah pek leh tur.
  8. Leitha pek rualin uluk taka rihvur tur a ni.

Sik leh Sa

  1. Fur laiin awih tlanah chin tur a ni.
  2. Thlasikah tlangsang leh hmun zo emaw phaizawlah pawh a chin theih lamah chin tur a ni.
  3. A chi tha taka a tiah theihna chu 13 0C-160C.
  4. Boruak vawt tak pawh a tuar zo, hnawng bawk se, tuia tam luttuk chuan a hlum a keh thei.

A chi mamawh zat

Thar theih zat

  1. Acre 1 hmunah 130 - 180 qtl a thar thei a, Hybrid phei chu 280-320qtl lai a thar thei.
  2. Thar rang hi ni 60 - 80 ah a puitling a, thar tlai chu ni 100 -120 velah a puitling.

Variety
